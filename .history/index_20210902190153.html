The Impact of Technology in Japan:

The modern technology that Japan has started when Meiji Restoration happened. The Meiji restoration was when the Japanese were freed from their emperor and could leave the country. With the help of some other countries, Japan grew a lot. There was a large increase in military power. Planes, cars, and railways were taking off. In fact, from 1872 to 1914 the length in miles of train tracks went from 18 to 7,100 which is a 7,082 increase.

Tokyo, one of the biggest cities in Japan also had a big impact on technology. The first subway line in Japan was completed on December 30, 1927. Then in 1978 a skyscraper called Sunshine 60 was built which was the tallest in Asia back then. Also, the Narita International Airport was built. That’s when the Tokyo subway became one of the busiest in the world.
